The Journey

A Dream in the Making

Neo has been selected to travel to Madrid, Spain to train with the prestigious Real Madrid Foundation. This selection is not just a testament to his skill on the field, but his dedication, discipline, and passion for the beautiful game.


To support this incredible opportunity, we hosted a community cornhole fundraiser at Fun Bags Cornhole Lounge. We continue to invite local businesses to partner with us as sponsors.


This is more than just a trip; it's a chance for a young athlete to represent our community on an international stage. By sponsoring, you gain positive local visibility among families and community leaders while helping make a dream come true.
